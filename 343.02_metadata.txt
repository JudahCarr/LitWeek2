Data citation:

NOAA/CDO, 2023: GHCND Raw Temperature Data via NOAA/National Climatic Data Center, accessed 2024-01-27 from: https://www.ncdc.noaa.gov/cdo-web/. Subsets used: 
+ Richmond International Airport, Richmond, VA, USA; 01-March-1939 to 31-December-2023

This dataset contains weather variables for:
-STATION: NOAA weather station coade
-NAME: Longform weather station name
-DATE: Date of observation in YYYY-MM-DD
-PRCP: Daily total precipitation in inches
-TMAX: Daily maximum temperature in °F
-TMIN: Daily minimum temperature in °F
